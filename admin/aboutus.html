-About-

Kindergarten Courseware is an Electronic learning sytem that offers
basic learning materials for children and young ages. It will give the little
ones an idea on what is being taught or what will be learn in school hence,
it will not be difficult for them to cope up with the lessons. As a fulfilment
for the requirements as graduating students, we choose this System for our thesis
because we want to offer the kids something they will enjoy and at the same time
will educate them as they face with their computers. We hope that through this,
we can make parents comfortable in seeing their children
interacting with computers
